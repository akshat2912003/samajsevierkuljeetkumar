Kuljeet Kumar

Engineer & Social Worker from Bihar

Kuljeet Kumar is a well-known Executive Engineer and social worker from Bihar. He has been actively working in Bhojpur and Bokaro for more than 10 years, contributing to education, women empowerment, rural development, and disaster relief.

Kuljeet Kumar Bihar is widely known for his contribution to social work and engineering services in rural areas.

Also known as Engineer Kuljeet Kumar, he has helped thousands of people through social initiatives including free education, marriage support, and village development.

कुलजीत कुमार
